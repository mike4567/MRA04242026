# **App Name**: ChipFix

## Core Features:

- AI Damage Assessment: Use an AI model, as a tool, to analyze uploaded images and determine the type and severity of windshield damage, suggesting one of three outcomes: 'Repairable,' 'Needs Human Evaluation,' or 'Replacement Recommended.'
- Image Upload: Allow users to upload images of their windshield damage (inside and outside views) for AI analysis.
- Assessment Display: Display the AI assessment results and recommended next steps clearly to the user.
- Contact Information Form: Enable users to input their contact info (Name, Address).
- Confirmation Page: Provide users with a summary of their selected date, time, and inputted contact details
- Availability Management: Provide an interface where the administrator can define appointment availability by setting open slots and blocking out specific dates and times.
- Appointments List View: A central view displaying a list of all scheduled appointments. Key details should be visible at a glance: Appointment Date/Time, Customer Name, Address, and the current Appointment Status.
- Appointment Detail View: Clicking on any appointment from the list should open a detailed view. In this view, an administrator must be able to: View all submitted information, including the original damage image. Edit the appointment date and time. Delete the appointment entirely. Update the appointment's status.
- Status Management: The appointment status must be a selectable option with the following states: Evaluation (Default status for all new appointments) Scheduled (Technician has reviewed and confirmed the appointment) Pending (Technician is en-route or actively working on the repair) Complete (The repair has been finished)
- Automated Email Notifications: Implement automated email triggers that send a notification to the user whenever the appointment status is updated by the administrator (e.g., when changed from Evaluation to Scheduled, Scheduled to Pending, and Pending to Complete).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#2E5A88), reflecting the clarity of glass and a sense of reliability.
- Background color: Light blue-gray (#E0E5EA), creating a clean, professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Warm orange (#E57A4A) for CTAs and important notifications, providing contrast and a sense of urgency.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif font for a clean, modern, readable interface.
- Use simple, geometric icons to represent assessment results and appointment details.
- Maintain a clean, minimalist layout to emphasize ease of use and clear communication.
